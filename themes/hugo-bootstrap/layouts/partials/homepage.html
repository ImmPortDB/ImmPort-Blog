ImmPort is funded by the NIH, NIAID and DAIT in support of the NIH mission to share data with the public. It is a long-term, sustainable data warehouse developed for the purpose of promoting re-use of immunological data generated by NIAID DAIT and DMID funded investigators. Data shared through ImmPort has been provided by NIH-funded programs, other research organizations and individual scientists ensuring these discoveries will be the foundation of future research.

The ImmPort ecosystem provides advanced information technology support in the production, analysis, archiving, and exchange of scientific data for the diverse community of life science researchers supported by NIAID DAIT DMID and other NIH and non-government funding agencies.
